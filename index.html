Registration and Pricing

The price of registration for Idividuals is ₹1000 and price of registration for School Delegation ₹2500

Registration will be only online

Payment will be accepted online or onspot

Dress Code

Boys: Formal solid color trouser, formal shirt, tie and blazer (optional), formal belt and shoes. Hair should be neatly combed.

Girls: Formal knee length skirt, formal shirt,formal closed shoes. Hair should be neatly tied.

Venue:

The Bangalore OakJMun will be held at the Oakridge International Bangalore Campus
