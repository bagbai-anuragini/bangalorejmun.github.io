How to Register?

Step 1: Pay through the link provided under the registration tab.

Step 2: Note the transaction ID, it will be used to fill the form.

Step 3: Open the registration form and fill in the relevant details along with the transaction ID.

Step 4: Await authentication from the school.

Step 5: Now that the school has registered itself, the individual delegates can register by using the individual application form.

Fee Structure

INR 2500 for the school's registration fee.

INR 1000 for each individual delegate's registration fee.

Payment Details

The registration fee is inclusive of the following:

Conference Fee - Including delegate Kit

Meals - Lunch, Snacks, tea and coffee on all the days of the conference

Preparation - Access to pre-conference training, background guides, and preparation guides

What is JMUN?

Junior Model United Nations is a platform for students from 6th to 8th grade to engage in substantive debate through a simulation of the United Nations.

group

Delegation

With over 200 delegates, the third edition of this conference is set to be the largest Junior MUN in Bangalore.
