Redefining MUN

As the first Junior Model UN conference, Bangalore JMUN has striven to redefine Model United Nations in Bangalore. For the past three years, we have continuously improved the quality of our debate, expanded the scope of our committees, and offered delegates a more holistic conference experience.

This year, Bangalore Junior Model United Nations has committed to providing a seamless conference-to-audience connection through our Facebook Page livestreams, improved conference experience, and a more engaging experience for all delegates.
