    Bangalore Junior MUN

    Bangalore JMUN, a conference hosted by Oakridge International School, is the only Junior Model United Nations Conference in Bangalore. Since the conference began in 2016, Bangalore JMUN has come a long way in providing an enriching experience to middle school students with a passion for debate and public speaking. This conference is an amazing opportunity for sixth to eighth graders to take their communication and leadership skills to the next level by collaborating with fellow students to solve 21st century global issues. The dedicated and experienced Executive Board and Secretariat at the conference, who strive to provide the best learning experience for the delegates, make it the best Junior Model United Nations Conference in Bangalore!

    Oakridge International School

    Oakridge International School, starting out not more than a decade ago, has become the largest chain of IB schools in India. The teaching methodology, the integration of technology and the myriad of opportunities provided to students have made Oakridge one of the best international schools in the country. The vision to nurture the uniqueness in every child has made it an excellent centre of education. From a variety of fields, unique experiences such as this are provided to students, making them get a holistic educational experience.

    Model United Nations

    Model United Nations is a platform for students to understand how the United Nations works through the lens of a global leader. Students represent countries, participate in UN committees and collectively formulate solutions to global problems, that have the potential to help humanity. This experience is unique as not only do students develop public speaking, negotiating, leadership and communication skills, but they also interact with like minded people and form friendships that go a long way in their lives.

    What is UN4MUN?

    UN4MUN was created by the UN Department for Public Information as a set of guidelines for conferences to better emulate the proceedings and structure of the United Nations. This year, Bangalore JMUN 2019 has adopted these new Rules of Procedure to better facilitate the flow of debate and enable students to engage with a more realistic simulation of the United Nations. Rest assured, delegates will get the opportunity to learn about the UN4MUN Rules of Procedure at this year's training session!
