How to Register?

Step 1: Pay through the link provided under the registration tab.

Step 2: Note the transaction ID, it will be used to fill the form.

Step 3: Open the registration form and fill in the relevant details along with the transaction ID.

Step 4: Await authentication from the school.

Fee Structure

INR 2500 for the school's registration fee.

INR 1000 for each individual delegate's registration fee.
