Registration

The registration fee for the school is 2500 INR, while the price of participation for each individual delegate is 1000 INR.

Registration can be done online, while the payment may be made online or in the school.

Dress Code

Boys: Formal solid color trouser, formal shirt, tie and blazer (optional), formal belt and shoes. Hair should be neatly combed.

Girls: Formal knee length skirt, formal shirt,formal closed shoes. Hair should be neatly tied.
