Economic and Social Council

Agenda: Unsustainable Consumption of Non-Renewable Resources

“The future is green energy, sustainable, renewable energy.” - Arnold Schwarzenegger

Energy is being used all around us, it powers all the things we see around us. Currently most of this energy comes from nonrenewable sources of energy and one day if these sources of energy run out the world as we know it will collapse. With the continued exploitation of fossil fuels like petroleum and coal, this day could be in the near future. Before we wake up on that dreadful day, the world needs to adopt sustainable energy resources.

As delegates of ECOSOC you will be addressing Sustainable Development Goal 12 - Responsible Consumption and Production by discussing the unsustainable consumption of non-renewable resources.
