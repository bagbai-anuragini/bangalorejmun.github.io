Disarmament and Security Council

Agenda: Global Co-operation in Regards To Nuclear Proliferation

The disarmament and international security committee is the first committee of the general assembly and deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime.

DISEC will be discussing the proliferation of illicit arms trade in lesser developed economies. The illicit proliferation and misuse of small arms and light weapons ranks among today’s most pressing security threats. Tens of thousands of people are killed or wounded each year in conflicts that are fought primarily with these weapons.

As delegates of DISEC you will be fighting this century old problem that manifests itself in new forms.
