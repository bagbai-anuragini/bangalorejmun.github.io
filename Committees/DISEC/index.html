How to Register?

Step 1: Pay through the link provided under the registration tab.

Step 2: Note the transaction ID, it will be used to fill the form.

Step 3: Open the registration form and fill in the relevant details along with the transaction ID.

Step 4: Await authentication from the school.

Step 5: Now that the school has registered itself, the individual delegates can register by using the individual application form.

Fee Structure

INR 2500 for the school's registration fee.

INR 1000 for each individual delegate's registration fee.

Payment Details

The registration fee is inclusive of the following:

Conference Fee - Including delegate Kit

Meals - Lunch, Snacks, tea and coffee on all the days of the conference

Preparation - Access to pre-conference training, background guides, and preparation guides

Disarmament and Security Committee

Global Co-operation in Regards To Nuclear Proliferation

The Disarmament and International Security Committee is the first committee of the General Assembly and deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime.

The threat to the world posed by nuclear weapons has grown considerably in recent times.

This year at BangaloreJMUN the delegates of the DISEC committee will be debating and discussing the issue of nuclear proliferation, and how to come to a peaceful consensus with regards to it. The delegates of the DISEC committee will be working towards achieving two Sustainable Development Goals; Goal 16 and Goal 17.
