Registration Prodecure

Step 1) The fee can be paid using the payment button in the registration menu.

Step 2) Note the transaction ID to use in the form.

Step 3) Open the registration form and fill in the relevant details and the transaction ID from the previous payment.

Step 4) Await authentication from the school.

Step 5) Receive the respective allocations at a later date.

Fee Structure

INR 2500 for the school's registration fee.

INR 1000 for each individual delegate's registration fee.
